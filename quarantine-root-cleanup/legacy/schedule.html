GAME RACE SETTINGS
  • Rider flagging is disabled for ZRL races.
  • Points and Scratch races offer a custom selection of powerups issued at various arches throughout the races.
  • Powerups are disabled during Team Time Trials
  • Bike upgrades are enabled.
  • TT bikes offer draft during TTT events but are disabled for Points and Scratch races.
  • Steering and braking are disabled for ZRL races.
