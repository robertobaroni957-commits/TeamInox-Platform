Team Members

ZWIFT->WTRL CONNECTION
As of June 2025, all members must CONNECT their WTRL.racing profile with Zwift. An orange WTRL logo will be visible at Zwift.com/connections. Should a user need to reconnect, they can do this from their WTRL.racing Profile page by clicking the ORANGE Zwift Authenticated button. This is a legal requirement from Zwift to ensure compliance with data-sharing laws.
If you are reading this message, you have already completed the process.

Unauthenticated WTRL members will need to:

  1. Login to WTRL.racing —> auto-redirected to Zwift.
  2. Login to Zwift
  3. Click “Yes” to data sharing —> auto-returned to WTRL.racing.
  4. Done!
It takes about 30 seconds to complete and only then will members become available for team selection.
